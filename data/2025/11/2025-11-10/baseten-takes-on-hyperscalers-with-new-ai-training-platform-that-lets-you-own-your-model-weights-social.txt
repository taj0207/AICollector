Baseten, a San Francisco-based AI infrastructure company valued at $2.15 billion, is reshaping enterprise AI with its new training platform. This innovative tool allows businesses to fine-tune open-source AI models without the operational burdens of managing complex infrastructure. CTO Amir Haghighat emphasizes that customer demand drove this pivotal shift, as companies seek to reduce reliance on proprietary systems like OpenAI. Unlike competitors, Baseten ensures users retain ownership of their model weights, promoting flexibility and control. With features like multi-cloud GPU orchestration and rapid job scheduling, Baseten is positioned to lead in the evolving AI landscape, empowering enterprises to deploy custom models efficiently and effectively.
