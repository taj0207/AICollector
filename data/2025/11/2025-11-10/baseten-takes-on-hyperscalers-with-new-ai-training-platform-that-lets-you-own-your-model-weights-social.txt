Baseten, valued at $2.15 billion, has pivoted to AI training with its new platform, Baseten Training. This infrastructure solution empowers companies to fine-tune open-source AI models without the hassle of managing GPU clusters or cloud planning. CTO Amir Haghighat highlights the urgent demand from customers tired of operational headaches. Unlike competitors, Baseten allows businesses to retain ownership of their model weights, fostering flexibility and control. As open-source models gain traction, Baseten aims to reshape enterprise AI deployment, ensuring companies can efficiently transition from training to inference without the burdens of traditional systems. This could be a game-changer in the evolving landscape of AI. Read more about it on VentureBeat.
