Baseten, valued at $2.15 billion, is reshaping AI training with its new platform, Baseten Training. This infrastructure allows companies to fine-tune open-source AI models without the hassle of managing GPU clusters or cloud capacity. As enterprises seek to reduce reliance on closed-source providers like OpenAI, Baseten offers control over model weights and a streamlined experience. CTO Amir Haghighat emphasizes the importance of learning from past failures, ensuring this new approach meets customer needs effectively. With features like multi-node training and dynamic GPU provisioning, Baseten aims to empower businesses to optimize AI deployment seamlessly.
