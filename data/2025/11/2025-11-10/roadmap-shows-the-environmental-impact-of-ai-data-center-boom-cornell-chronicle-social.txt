A new report highlights the significant environmental impact of the booming AI data center industry. As demand for AI technologies grows, so does the energy consumption of these facilities, raising concerns about sustainability. The findings urge stakeholders to prioritize eco-friendly practices in the design and operation of data centers. With the future of AI hanging in the balance, it's crucial to balance innovation with environmental responsibility. Read more about this pressing issue in the Cornell Chronicle.
