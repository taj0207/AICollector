A new report highlights the significant environmental toll of the AI data center boom. As demand for AI technologies surges, so does the energy consumption of these facilities, raising urgent questions about sustainability. The 'Roadmap' outlines the challenges and potential solutions, urging stakeholders to consider greener alternatives in the race to innovate. The future of AI must balance technological advancement with environmental responsibility. Read more about this crucial issue in the Cornell Chronicle.
