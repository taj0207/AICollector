Enter Qodo, an AI tool that transformed their workflow by providing context-aware insights on pull requests. Qodo's ability to learn from existing code and team practices makes it feel like an additional teammate, enhancing collaboration and efficiency. The results? Significant time savings and a more robust codebase, showcasing the power of context engineering in modern software development.
