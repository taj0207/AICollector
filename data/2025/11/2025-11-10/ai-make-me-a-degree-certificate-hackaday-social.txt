The rise of AI has sparked a new trend: creating fake degree certificates. As technology advances, so do the methods for deception. This phenomenon raises questions about authenticity in education and the implications for employers. With a few clicks, anyone can generate a convincing document, challenging the integrity of academic achievements. It's a reminder that while AI offers incredible possibilities, it also presents significant ethical dilemmas that society must confront.
