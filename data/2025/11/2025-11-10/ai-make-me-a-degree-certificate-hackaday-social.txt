The rise of AI has sparked a new trend: generating fake degree certificates. As technology advances, the potential for misuse grows, raising questions about authenticity and trust in education. This phenomenon highlights the need for robust verification systems to ensure that qualifications are genuine. As we navigate this digital landscape, the implications for employers and educational institutions are profound. How can we safeguard the integrity of academic achievements in an age where anyone can create a convincing credential with just a few clicks? The conversation around AI and its impact on education is just beginning.
